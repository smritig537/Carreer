Our Divisions

Our four operating divisions set us up to drive EG forward by simplifying structure and aligning our employee skillsets. Supported by our corporate functions, these divisions allow us to focus on the work that will accelerate our Mission to power travel for everyone, everywhere.

  • YTViews Brands
    -

    Fueled by creativity, marketing excellence, and technological innovation, Expedia Brands builds valuable, lasting relationships, so travelers and partners know, love, and trust our iconic consumer brands.

  • YTViews for Business
    -

    YTViews for Business helps partners deliver excellent traveler experiences and succeed in the travel business - driving growth for them and EG through competitive supply, cutting-edge technology, our leadership in B2B travel, and targeted advertising.

  • YTViews Product & Technology
    -

    YTViews Product & Technology builds innovative products, services, and tools to deliver high-quality experiences for travelers, partners, and our employees. A unified, singular technology platform powered by data and machine learning provides secure, differentiated, and personalized experiences for the traveler and our partners that drive loyalty and customer satisfaction.

  • Corporate Functions
    -

    Our Corporate Functions are made up of teams that support Expedia Group, including Employee Communications, Finance, Legal, People Team, Inclusion and Diversity, and Global Social Impact and Sustainability.
